# Part 1: Understanding the Business

Ummahpreneur is a platform dedicated to supporting Muslim entrepreneurs by providing resources and community engagement focused on Sharia-compliant business practices. The platform emphasizes connecting Muslim founders with mentors and peers to foster growth without compromising Islamic values. Through social media engagement, particularly on Instagram and TikTok, Ummahpreneur offers educational content such as strategy calls and podcasts featuring successful Muslim entrepreneurs. These resources aim to help users navigate the complexities of Islamic finance and entrepreneurship.

Ummahpreneur serves a diverse customer segment primarily consisting of Muslim tech startups and entrepreneurs seeking to scale their businesses while adhering to Islamic principles. This group often includes individuals like Ahmed, a 34-year-old tech consultant in London, who values structured mentorship that aligns with his religious and business goals.
